Black Ops 2 is one of the best games in the Call of Duty series. It is an action packed first person shooter with great campaign, zombies, and multiplayer modes.

Mutliplayer Zombies Campaign
Multiplayer has a multitude of game modes that engage players into objective based games.
Some of the game modes inclue Team Deathmatch, Demolition, Search and Destroy, etc.
There is a progression system that helps players gain achievements for playing the game.		 The zombies mode has 5 maps Tranzit, Die Rise, Mob of the Dead, Origins,and Buried.
Each map has its own unique features that allow the player to have a different experience every game.
The Zombies mode is great for casual players who like killing zombies and solving puzzles!		 Campaign is action packed with missions played in two different eras futuristic and past
The main character's name is David Mason and you're goal is to kill Raul Menendez in 2025.
