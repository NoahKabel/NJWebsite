League of Legends sets the bar for strategy games!

League of Legends is a pc multiplayer game with 143 champions that battle in a 3 lane map.
The objective of the game is to defeat enemy champions, troops, and towers/life support.

Tank Fighter Mage
Tanks are the champions that sacrifice health for crowd control.
Tanks are useful for disrupting the enemy team so that teammates can advance or retreat.
But Tanks are vulnerable to fighters and marksmen which will kill them with ease if ignored.		 Fighters are champions that excel at dealing and surviving damage.
Fighters are great at extended fights which happen quite often in a single match.
Fighters are vulnerable to mages and marksmen so be careful of them!		 Mages are champions that have great range, area based damage and crowd control.
Mages are useful for the team because they have great combos that deal heavy damage and kill enemies.
However mages are vulnerable to assasins and tanks

Clash royale is the perfect game for gamers away from home!

Clash royale is a mobile game created by Supercell where you create a deck of cards and battle against other players in real time.
To win you need to get 3 crowns or have more crowns than the opponent when the time runs out. Each person has 3 towers, One strong center tower and two weaker side towers.
If you take down one of the side towers you get one crown and if you take down the center tower you get 3 crowns and win.

1v1 2v2 Special events
In 1v1 battles you will face off against one other player. If you win then you gain trophies, and if you lose you loe trophies. In 2v2 battles you and a teamate will face of against 2 other people. Winning and losing does not affect trophies in 2v2. Sometimes they have special events
